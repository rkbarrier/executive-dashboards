# Technology Resiliency Executive Dashboard

## Executive Summary

This sample executive dashboard demonstrates how technology resiliency metrics can be presented to senior leadership. The purpose is to provide a concise view of organizational readiness, business continuity performance, disaster recovery preparedness, and operational risk.

> **Portfolio Note:** This dashboard is an original example created for professional portfolio purposes and does not contain confidential or proprietary information.
